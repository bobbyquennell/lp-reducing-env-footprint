WHAT IS THE PEACE PLAY PROJECT?

Together with International Alert, our mission this Christmas is to send love, joy and compassion to some of the most vulnerable people in the world.

The Peace Play Project provides organised, therapeutic play sessions to help traumatised children to heal and express difficult emotions. Sessions are determined by the moods of the children, and can involve playback theatre as a way of encouraging children to workd through the trauma they've faced.

Every gift you buy from our seasonal gift collections will help fund the Peace Play Project in Lebanon for a year, providing play and activities in a safe place for Syrian refugee children to paly and heal.

HELP CHILDREN LIKE GHARAM AND LEENA

Gharam and her family are from Aleppo. Her favourite colours are red and yellow and she dreams of becoming a doctor. When asked about The Peace Play Project, she said "I love it, A LOT". This is the only place for her to play safely and make friends. Gharam gets very excited for every session.

Leena's family fled Syria with only the clothes they were wearing. She has been in camp for five years now. It's crowded and the streets are too dangerous to play outside. The peace Play Project is the only time Leena gets to play with other children outside of ther family and become the outgoing little girl she is.

WHO ARE INTERNATIONAL ALERT?

International Alert works with people directly affected by conflict to build lasting peace. They focus on solving the root causes of conflict with people from across divides. From the grassrootd to policy level, they work in collaboration turning in-depth research into practical solutions that make a difference on the ground.
